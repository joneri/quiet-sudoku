# Privacy Policy

macSudoku does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Data Collection

macSudoku does not use analytics, tracking, advertising identifiers, third-party SDKs, accounts, sign-in, or cloud services.

## Local Data

The app may save game progress, settings, and high score initials locally on your Mac so the game can restore your board and show the local leaderboard.

This data stays on your device and is not sent to the developer or any third party.

## Network Access

macSudoku does not require network access for gameplay.
